History

This retro style restaurant was built in 1984 and somehow for better or for worst we just never lost that 80s charm while giving people what they want...the best pizza and wings around! Being based in New York we had absolutely no choice but to have the best New York style pizza around and just to rub it into any other possible competitor we added wings to the mix. The only way this could be better is if we were in Manhatten, well guess you're in luck because we are in Manhatten.

You could also say we never group up considering our 80s aesthetic has not changed one bit.
